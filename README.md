# ⚓ PFA Pro™ | Mission Readiness Command

**PFA Pro™** is the premier tactical coach for the U.S. Navy Physical Fitness Assessment. Built for 2026 standards, it bridges the gap between BCA compliance and performance-driven logistics. No more guessing—just mission-ready results.

## 🚀 New in V2.0: Tactical Logistics & Sourcing
* **Zip-Code Optimized Sourcing:** Enter your **Zip Code** to identify nearby provisioning options. The AI optimizes your list for the **Commissary, Costco, Sam's Club, Walmart**, and more. 
* **14-Day Logistics Sync:** High-intensity training requires high-intensity fueling. PFA Pro now generates **2-week consolidated shopping lists** synchronized with your specific 14-day workout evolution.
* **Pantry Intelligence:** Inventory your "locker" (pantry) so the AI only generates a list of what you *actually* need to buy, saving money and time.
* **Performance Deep-Dives:** Training blocks that adapt to your specific equipment availability (Full Gym vs. Bodyweight) and PRT goals.

## 🛠️ Core Capabilities
* **BCA Precision:** Hard-coded Navy Max Weight tables and Waist-to-Height ratio logic (2026 Standards).
* **AI Nutrition Log:** Log meals in plain English; the AI estimates calories and logs them to your local history.
* **Meal Prep Measurements:** Converts performance goals into actionable food targets to ensure you are fueled for the PRT.
* **Recovery Tracking:** Integrated monitoring to ensure sleep and mobility support your training volume.
